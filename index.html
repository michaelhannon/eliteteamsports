05 / FAQ

Questions, answered.

Every member gets a personal keypad code. You can come in anytime: 5am, midnight, holidays. The facility is monitored by security cameras, and lights and machines are coin-free for members.
Cages are first-come, first-served for members during normal hours, with the option to reserve a cage in our member app during peak times. Team practices and lessons are booked in advance.
From t-ball kids through college players and adult rec leagues. Our pitching machines start at slow-pitch speeds and dial up to 85+ mph for the older guys. Softball settings too.
Family memberships include up to 4 household members. Individual members get a few guest passes per month, perfect for showing your buddy what they're missing before they sign up.
Memberships are month-to-month with no long-term contract. Cancel any time with 30 days notice. We earn your business every month.
Members book lessons directly through our app or by calling the facility. Browse coach availability, pick a time, and we'll send a confirmation. Member pricing applies automatically.
