// 01Introduction

Elite Team Sports ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard information when you visit our website, become a member, book lessons, or otherwise interact with our facility and services.

By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our website or services.

Plain English
We collect what we need to run a batting cage facility: sign-ups, payments, lesson bookings. And we don't sell your information. Read on for the details.

// 02Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account & Membership: Name, email address, mailing address, phone number, date of birth, emergency contact, and account credentials.
  • Family/Team Members: Names, ages, and emergency contacts of family members or team rosters added to your membership.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, billing address, and transaction history. Payment cards are processed through our third-party payment processor; we do not store full card numbers on our systems.
  • Lesson & Booking Details: Coach preferences, lesson types, scheduled times, and notes related to training.
  • Communications: Information you provide when you contact us, submit feedback, or sign up for newsletters.
  • Waivers & Releases: Liability waivers, photo/video releases, and any medical information you choose to share.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, pages viewed, referring URLs, and approximate location.
  • Facility Access Logs: Keypad entry codes and timestamps for facility access (used for security and account verification).
  • Video Surveillance: Our facility uses security cameras for safety. Footage is retained for a limited time and reviewed only when necessary.
  • Cookies: See the Cookies & Tracking section below.

// 03How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your membership account
  • Process payments and renewals
  • Schedule, confirm, and manage lessons and facility bookings
  • Communicate about your account, bookings, events, and policy updates
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Improve our website, services, and facility
  • Maintain facility security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Send marketing communications (only with your consent, and you can unsubscribe anytime)

// 04Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share information only as follows:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties that help us operate the business (payment processors, booking software, email platforms, security systems). They are contractually required to handle data only on our behalf.
  • Coaches & Instructors: Names and lesson notes are shared with the coaches you book sessions with.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Elite Team Sports, our members, or others.
  • Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • With Your Consent: Any other sharing will be done only with your explicit permission.

// 05Children & Minors

Many of our members are minors. We require a parent or legal guardian to register, manage the membership, and provide consent for any information about a minor. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children under 13 without parental consent, and we limit the information we collect about minors to what is reasonably necessary for their participation.

Parents and guardians may review, update, or request deletion of their child's information by contacting us.

// 06Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, keep you logged in, understand how the site is used, and measure marketing effectiveness. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect site functionality.

We may also use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) and advertising pixels. These third parties collect information about your visits in accordance with their own privacy policies.

// 07Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

// 08Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing
  • Receive a portable copy of your information

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below. We will verify your identity before responding and will reply within the timeframes required by applicable law.

// 09Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will post the updated policy on this page with a new effective date and, when appropriate, notify you by email or through our website. Your continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
